About Ron Kamonohashi

Ron Kamonohashi: Deranged Detective is about Ron, a detective who was forced to leave behind solving cases after he was framed for a crime years ago in his old academy. However, when well meaning but dim witted detective Toto stumbles his way, they become unlikely allies, as Toto becomes an opportunity for Ron to follow his passion of doing detective work once again.

About the Author

Akira Amano is a mangaka who is renowned namely for the following works:
Katekyo Hitman Reborn!, Ron Kamonohashi: Deranged Detective, and her character designs in Psycho-Pass.

She was born in 1973, and currently lives in Japan.
