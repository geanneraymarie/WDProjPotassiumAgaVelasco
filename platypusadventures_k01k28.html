Dive into the world of Ron Kamonohashi: Deranged Detective!

In this fanmade project, you will step into Ron's shoes, going through different levels of the cases he faced in the series. You will be seeing the story from Ron's perspective, offering deeper immersion into the story in the form of a mystery solving game, brought to you by 2 devoted developers.

Do well to solve the mystery and catch the culprit before it's too late . . .
