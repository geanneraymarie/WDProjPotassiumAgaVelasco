About Ron Kamonohashi

Ron Kamonohashi: Deranged Detective is about Ron, who was forced to stopped solving cases after he was framed for a crime years ago in his old academy. However, when well meaning but dim witted detective Toto stumbles his way, they become unlikely allies, as Toto becomes an opportunity for Ron to follow his passion of doing detective work once again.

About the Author

Akira Amano is renowned namely for the following works: Katekyo Hitman Reborn!, Ron Kamonohashi: Deranged Detective, and her character designs in Psycho-Pass. She was born in 1973, and currently lives in Japan.

Characters

Ron is a playful genius who is also eccentric who has various strange interests. He has a deep passion for solving mysteries, believing that he should use his gift selflessly. He loves platypuses and brown sugar syrup. Toto is a cherished friend of his.

Ron Kamonohashi

Toto is an enthusiastic detective who is bad at putting clues together. He tends to pick up on clues but is ironically clueless on how to connect them. He's a kindhearted soul who would sacrifice his life to save others. He has saved Ron on multiple occasions.

Totomaru Isshiki

Milo is the head of the M. Family. He is a cunning criminal mastermind who has led the family for years, stopping at nothing to get to his position. He even killed his own sister to prove himself worthy. He has taken an interest in Ron due to a connection that haunts Ron's past.

Milo Moriarty

Winter is Milo's sibling. Their assigned sex at birth is female, but their gender identity is not confirmed. They are a master at disguises. They have a strained relationship with Milo because he killed their sister, Alice. They turn out to be neutral good later on in the series.

Winter Moriarty

Eliot is Ron's father. He was a loving father who gave Ron the freedom to live his life however he wanted, not bounded by his family ties to both Holmes and Moriarty. He changed after meeting Ron's mother. He was killed when Ron was six, which traumatized him and left his memories from the years up until then blurry.

Spitz is the tracking instructor at BLUE. He's a cheerful person who is also quite easily swayed by his emotions. Despite this, he is a reliable ally, who has helped Ron in many ways, one of which is by keeping his going back to solving cases a secret from BLUE.

Spitz Fire

Amamiya is Toto's boss. She is a capable police officer who works hard and is honest. Her first name was never revealed. She develops a small crush on Ron.
